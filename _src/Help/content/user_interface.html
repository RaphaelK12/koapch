KO Approach features a simple and intuitive user interface consisting of two primary components:

  • the Options window, containing the program's buttons and controls, and
  • the Tray Menu, providing quick access to most frequently used options of KO Approach.

The Options window can be accessed by:

  • double-clicking the Tray icon;
  • right-clicking the Tray icon and choosing Options... from the menu.

The Options window is subdivided into sections represented by the following tabs:

  • Features tab
  • Menus & Scrolling tab
  • Contents tab
  • Maintenance tab
  • Registration tab
  • About tab
